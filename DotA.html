DotA 2 (defence of the ancients 2) is a multiplayer 5v5 moba, there are 2 teams who fight to destroy the ancient of the other team.
spoiler: there is a third ancient called arc warden, but that is a playable character.
My most played hero is oracle, but i also like to play as techies, terrorblade, naga siren, meepo, invoker and ringmaster.
